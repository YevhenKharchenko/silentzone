Core Experience

Luma Drift places the player inside a constantly moving abstract environment. Instead of objectives or levels, the game responds to how smoothly and thoughtfully the player guides light streams through space. The focus is not on winning — it’s on maintaining flow.

The Experience

This is not a game about speed or precision.

Luma Drift encourages players to slow down and pay attention to motion, rhythm, and visual feedback.

Touch something and watch the colors bloom outward, hear the tones shift - no two sessions ever look or sound exactly the same.
